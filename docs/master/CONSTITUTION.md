# SAGE CONSTITUTION & MASTER ARCHITECTURE RECORDS

This constitution establishes the immutable principles, core laws, and guiding tenets governing the **SAGE Autonomous Continuity Runtime**. These records represent the definitive criteria for SAGE's operational and structural behavior.

---

## 1. SAGE Mission
The mission of SAGE is to serve as the permanent, zero-copy-paste, cross-platform continuity and orchestration layer that preserves institutional intelligence and engineering knowledge across developers, AI systems, and project sessions without dependency on transient chat histories.

---

## 2. Core Governance Laws

### 2.1 Knowledge Persistence Law
No critical engineering fact, design specification, or system milestone may reside solely in ephemeral conversation buffers. All valid institutional memory must be serialized, validated, and promoted to persistent, structured, and auditable storage backends (`sage_data/` or immutable checkpoints).

### 2.2 Validation Before Expansion Law
The SAGE system must prioritize stability, test coverage, and validation checks before extending or expanding codebase functionality. New features or capabilities can only be added to a verified, compiling, and healthy baseline with all tests passing perfectly.

---

## 3. Guiding Architectural Principles

### 3.1 Evidence Symmetry Principle
Every technical and architectural decision recorded inside the SAGE ledger must reference explicit, valid, and traceably linked memory objects as supporting evidence. SAGE cannot establish or record unsupported decisions.

### 3.2 Confidence Honesty Principle
Knowledge entered into SAGE is strictly labeled by confidence levels (`hypothesis`, `validated`, `archived`) following multi-rule quality validation. No entry can be promoted without satisfying schema audits, Substance checks, and lineage verifications.

### 3.3 Evolution Without Destruction Principle
When SAGE evolves, optimizes, or updates its submodules, it must preserve backward compatibility, ensure existing persistence models rehydrate cleanly, and never destroy verified operational lineages or structural indices.
