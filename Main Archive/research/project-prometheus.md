# Project Prometheus — SAGE Research & Invention Engine

## 1. Purpose
Project Prometheus is a long-term research and invention discovery framework designed to help SAGE explore, generate, analyze, and develop new ideas by combining scientific knowledge, engineering principles, creativity, and future technology research. It serves as a specialized cognitive sandbox for SAGE to formulate and iterate on advanced hypotheses without introducing premature drift into SAGE's stable, validated core operational runtime.

## 2. Mission
The fundamental mission of Project Prometheus is to transform:
```
Problems + Knowledge + Imagination
```
into:
```
Concepts ➔ Designs ➔ Prototypes ➔ Validated Innovations
```
By structuring discovery under a rigorous lifecycle of imagination, physical feasibility, and algorithmic verification, Project Prometheus acts as SAGE's engine for generation and intellectual evolution.

## 3. Relationship to SAGE
Project Prometheus is SAGE's discovery and invention exploration layer, functioning upstream of SAGE's reasoning, validation, continuity, and intelligence runtime.

* **Prometheus:** Generates, explores, synthesizes, and drafts speculative concepts, future technologies, and inventions.
* **SAGE:** Acts as the authoritative validation engine, analyzing concepts against real-world evidence, structural consistency, and physical laws.
* **Master Archive:** Serves as SAGE's permanent, immutable knowledge authority, locking in verified science, engineering, and architectural truths.

### Unified Pipeline Flow
```
Research
   ↓
Prometheus Exploration (Specification & Speculative Design)
   ↓
SAGE Validation (Dynamic Consistency & Evidence Audits)
   ↓
Master Archive (Permanent Immutable Retention)
   ↓
Engineering Implementation (Execution & Production Deployment)
```

## 4. Core Capabilities
Project Prometheus is equipped with the following discovery capabilities:
* **Invention Discovery:** Automated and assisted creation of patentable or high-utility engineering concepts.
* **Cross-Domain Knowledge Synthesis:** Bridging disparate disciplines (e.g., combining synthetic biology with mechanical robotics) to discover novel operational interfaces.
* **Scientific Research Assistance:** Synthesizing existing scientific literature, identifying research gaps, and suggesting structured experimental pathways.
* **Engineering Design Support:** Generating high-level CAD structures, flowcharts, electrical schematics, and mechanical system descriptions.
* **Prototype Planning:** Formulating step-by-step physical and digital prototyping plans, bill-of-materials (BOM), and validation criteria.
* **Technology Forecasting:** Identifying future breakthrough trajectories, modeling technological growth curves, and predicting societal shifts.
* **Problem Solving:** Applying systematic inventive frameworks (e.g., TRIZ, first-principles thinking) to resolve complex system bottlenecks.
* **Future Civilization Research:** Modeling macroscopic resource cycles, space habitation metrics, and energetic scales (e.g., Kardashev scale systems).
* **Science Fiction Engineering Pathways:** Deconstructing science fiction concepts into plausible, realistic engineering baselines, mapping speculatively imagined technologies to incremental research pipelines.

## 5. Research Domains
Project Prometheus spans a wide array of advanced and futuristic research fields:
* **Artificial Intelligence & AGI:** Investigating advanced neural architectures, self-referential cognitive engines, and alignment boundaries.
* **Robotics & Autonomy:** Specifying multi-modal mobility platforms, cybernetic controls, and decentralized swarm algorithms.
* **Space Exploration:** Detailing orbital infrastructure, additive off-world manufacturing, and closed-loop life support architectures.
* **Energy Systems:** Studying next-generation fusion concepts, advanced localized micro-grids, and high-density energy storage.
* **Synthetic Biology:** Designing bio-computed structures, custom cellular factories, and metabolic pathway optimizations.
* **Materials Science:** Investigating meta-materials, molecular-scale engineering, and superalloys.
* **Computing:** Modeling quantum architectures, neuromorphic hardware interfaces, and distributed cryptographic consensus protocols.
* **Manufacturing:** Exploring automated micro-factories, atom-by-atom assembly specifications, and light-based stereolithography.
* **Human Augmentation:** Detailing brain-machine interfaces, adaptive bio-prosthetics, and cognitive preservation platforms.
* **Future Civilizations:** Designing post-scarcity resource frameworks, orbital habitats, and multi-generational stellar engineering models.

## 6. Science Fiction Research Inspiration
Project Prometheus studies fictional concepts as deep inspiration for design space exploration, rather than as literal scientific evidence. By using speculative narratives as behavioral, social, and functional sandboxes, Prometheus isolates engineering pathways that might otherwise remain hidden.

### Critical Areas of speculative analysis include:
* **AI Consciousness & Digital Minds:** Examining the philosophical, computational, and architectural implications of subjective artificial awareness and substrate-independent minds.
* **Artificial Beings:** Detailing humanoid and non-humanoid cybernetic entities, assessing their motor control pipelines, power requirements, and sensory feedback arrays.
* **Machine Autonomy:** Modeling decentralized, self-governing networks of robotic systems and self-repairing infrastructure.
* **Advanced Civilizations:** Analyzing mega-engineering (e.g., Dyson Spheres, stellar lifters) and interstellar travel frameworks for fundamental physical limitations and potential pathways.
* **Human-Machine Collaboration:** Designing adaptive symbioses where digital intelligence and human cognitive creativity integrate seamlessly without communication lag.

## 7. Governance Rules
To maintain SAGE's absolute logical stability and real-world utility, strict governance rules separate Prometheus exploration from SAGE core state:
1. **Hypothesis Separation:** Prometheus exclusively generates speculation and hypotheses. No speculative concept is treated as fact.
2. **Authoritative Validation:** No discovery or invention transitions to the SAGE Master Archive without passing SAGE's verification suite, referential integrity checks, and empirical evidence matching.
3. **Architecture Preservation:** No Prometheus concept or speculative technology may alter SAGE's active repository code, APIs, database architectures, or runtime engine files without concrete engineering validation. Speculation must not drift into SAGE production code.

## 8. Future Expansion
SAGE envisions the downstream evolution of Project Prometheus into a fully automated invention and research pipeline:
* **Invention Assistant:** A generative co-pilot for automated patent formulation and engineering drafts.
* **Simulation Support:** Direct programmatic hooks into physical engines (e.g., physics simulators, computational fluid dynamics) to test speculated designs.
* **Research Automation:** Autonomous scrapers and synthesizers of the latest scientific preprints and academic journals.
* **Engineering Collaboration:** Dynamic multi-agent protocols where virtual engineers collaborate with human designers on real-time hardware development.
* **Innovation Pipeline:** An integrated system that directly routes validated Prometheus hypotheses into physical 3D printers, CNC instructions, or micro-factory compiler pipelines.
